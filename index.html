Welcome to the official FriendMyFriend website! This company was made in 2022 by Ishi Shimamoto and Nathan Vankirk

What we do

We make open-sourced and closed-sourced software for the same purpose of making your programming and computer use easier.
This means we make everything from programming languages, operating systems, code editors, to just small coding tool kits.
Anything to make every aspect of using a computer more easy is our goal.
